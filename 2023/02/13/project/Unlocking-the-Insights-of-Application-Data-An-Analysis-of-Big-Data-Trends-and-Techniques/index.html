Introduction

Briefly introduce the topic and the purpose of your article.

The propose of this analysis is to help students who are facing a critical issue - getting a job to discern the employment trend currently so that they can make the right choice in time.

Explain what you want to achieve through the analysis of big data about applications.

Firstly, I research the proportion of all education qualifications in the social employment demand, which we can realize which degrees are the options to gain the offer easily. Besides, the average salary level by academic qualification is required to know. In addition, we need to know the number of jobs by location in our country, so we can arrive some locations with more requirement to find a job. Last but not least, the regression analysis between the job experience and salary will be display.

Highlight the significance of big data in today’s world and why it’s relevant to your topic.

Big data has become increasingly significant in today’s world due to its ability to provide insights and solutions to a wide range of problems and challenges across different industries. The significance of big data lies in its potential to improve decision making, increase efficiency, and drive innovation.

Overall, my goal of this article is using the big data to lead jobhunter making better decisions.

Data Collection

Explain how you collected the data for the analysis.

I use Selenium, an open-source software suite of browser automation tools, to collect the data from employment website: https://www.zhipin.com. Selenium has lots of function for controlling web browsers so that it make extracting data from a web page easily.

Mention the source of the data and the methods used to collect it.

  1. A web drive was created for us to manipulate the browser.

  2. We can then search a job and extract all job item the website responses. After that, we use find_element method the Selenium provides to obtain the data such as job_name,job_area,salary,com_name,com_type,finance_stage,com_size,work_year,education,welfare

  3. After that, we can save the data in a file by importing csv package.

Provide an overview of the size, quality, and relevance of the data.

There are 1448 records in the file.

Explain why data cleaning and preprocessing are important for big data analysis.

Data cleaning and preprocessing are crucial steps in the big data analysis process because they ensure the quality and reliability of the data being analyzed. Without these steps, the results of the analysis may be incorrect or misleading, which can have serious consequences in a variety of applications.
